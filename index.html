Notion Integration Connect your data for cross-device sync

📋 How the Sync Works

The sync pulls your current debt balances from Notion into the dashboard. Your Notion token lives securely in Netlify as an environment variable — never in this page. The only thing you save here is your Debts database ID.

1
In your Netlify site, go to Site configuration → Environment variables and add: key NOTION_TOKEN, value = your Notion integration token. Then go to Deploys → Trigger deploy → Deploy site.
2
Make sure your Debts database is shared with your integration: open the database in Notion, click ··· → Add connections → Finance Dashboard.
3
Paste your Debts database ID below and hit Save. The ID is the 32-character string in the database URL after notion.so/. Then click ↻ Sync Notion in the top bar.

🔑 Database IDs

Your Notion token is stored in Netlify, not here. Just save your database IDs below.

Works without Notion too. All your data saves to this browser automatically between sessions. Notion sync just keeps balances current across devices.
